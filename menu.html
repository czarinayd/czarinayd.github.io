We work each and every morning, hand-shaping and perfecting our recipes so that you can have the freshest, quality products every day. Let's share this delicious day together.

Classic Bread - $4 per loaf

Freshly baked bread with our signature golden crust and soft, chewy middle. Our bread is baked fresh every morning and is made from the best, local ingredients. A variety of options are offered in-house including rye, wheat, country, soft buns (available in half dozens), ciabatta, and seasonal holiday breads. Bread for delivery can be made to order.

Filled Bread - $5 per half dozen

Mr. Fukuo's trademark pastries are warm hand-sized buns filled to perfection. Bread fillings are available in various flavors including cheese, cream, chocolate, coconut, red bean, fruit jellies, and seasonal holiday flavors. Flavors vary in-house depending on the day, but filled bread for delivery can be made to order.

Cookies - $10 per dozen

These traditional favorites are freshly baked every day and made from the finest ingredients. Cookies for delivery are always made to order and are available in five delicious flavors: chocolate chip, sugar, gingerbread, snickerdoodle, and peanutbutter. In-house cookies vary every day.

Cake - starting at $15 per order

Gutiokipanja's cakes are renowned throughout the Mieko area for their moist texture and elegant flavors. A limited amount of cakes are available in-house in six-inch diameter sizes. Cake orders are customized and decorated with your preferences highly in mind. Additional chrages may apply to larger cake orders.
