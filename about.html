WHO ARE WE?

We are Gutiokipanja.

And we are inspired every day by the joy and people of Mieko to bring you good, welcoming food. We believe that warm, homemade bread can remedy almost any off-day as long as it's served by people who care. To us, that's what matters most.

OUR HISTORY

Our story began 30 years ago at the same place in Mieko Gutiokipanja stands today. Mrs. Osono Sr. started with one goal: to bake fresh bread from fresh dough every day from the bakery. No artificial preservatives or short cuts, just love and hot ovens.

MEET THE STAFF

MRS. OSONO

Mrs. Osono is the third generation manager and owner of Gutiokipanja in her family. Her grandmother founded the bakery when Mrs. Osono was just a little girl, and ever since then she has had a passion for food and people.

MR. FUKUO

Mr. Fukuo is the main baker at Gutiokipanja. He has been baking breads and pastries with his wife for the past 20 years. Mr. Fukuo's specialty is his coconut cream-filled breads.
